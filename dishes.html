Our Special Indian Dishes

Butter Chicken (Punjab)

Indulge in the creamy richness of butter chicken, a North Indian classic from Punjab with a spiced tomato gravy.

Biryani (Hyderabad)

A fragrant rice dish layered with tender spiced meat, often chicken or lamb, a flavorful treasure from Hyderabad.

Vada Pav (Maharashtra)

A popular street food from Mumbai, consisting of a spiced potato fritter served in a bun with chutneys.

Masala Dosa (Tamil Nadu)

A crispy, golden crepe made from fermented rice and lentils, filled with spiced potatoes, served with chutneys.

Chole Bhature (Punjab)

A signature punjabi dish made with Chole, infused with exotic spices. It is served with soft Bhature.

Paneer Tikka (Punjab)

A crispy,cubes of paneer marinated with variety of indian spices, served with a variety of chutneys.
